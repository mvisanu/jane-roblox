# Game Concept: Wildwood Adventure Town

## Game Vision

Create a cozy adventure building game for children,
especially girls who love exploration, creativity, teamwork,
and adventure.

This is NOT a princess game.

The player is not a princess waiting for rescue.

The player is:
- Explorer
- Builder
- Inventor
- Animal Friend
- Adventure Leader

The fantasy:
"Every child can build a world they are proud of."

---

# Target Audience

Primary:
- Kids age 6-12
- Girls and boys who enjoy creativity and adventure

Player personality:
- Curious
- Brave
- Imaginative
- Likes teamwork
- Loves animals
- Enjoys building and exploring

---

# Core Gameplay Loop

Explore → Collect → Build → Customize → Share → Adventure Again


## 1. Build Your Own Town

Players start with a small campsite.

Upgrade into:

- Tree house
- Wooden cottage
- Workshop
- Animal shelter
- Café
- Garden
- Adventure center

Players can customize:
- Furniture
- Decorations
- Building style
- Garden
- Outdoor areas


---
